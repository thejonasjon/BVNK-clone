    Frequently asked questions.

    How is BVNK licensed?
    add

    BVNK is regulated as an EMI in the UK and Europe and holds multiple VASP registrations in Europe. Please note that cryptoassets are not regulated in the UK by the FCA. We're committed to expanding our licensing footprint globally. Learn more about our licenses

    How much does BVNK’s platform cost?
    add

    We charge fees to merchants for using our services and our pricing tiers are based on payment volumes. Our aim is to operate with fair and transparent margins and to help you succeed financially. Please get in touch with our team to receive a bespoke pricing proposal.

    How does BVNK work?
    add

    BVNK is a payments platform for businesses. You can apply for a BVNK account today for free. Once you’re verified, you can hold funds, convert between currencies, send and receive payments in fiat or crypto, or add crypto payments to your checkout. Businesses pay fees to process payments through our platform.

    What can I do with BVNK’s platform?
    add

    With BVNK, businesses can send and receive stablecoin payments, convert between currencies and add crypto payments to their checkout. Our platform and global licensing empower innovators to launch new payments products compliantly.

    Who is BVNK for?
    add

    BVNK provides modern payments infrastructure for businesses, including financial institutions, payment institutions, trading platforms and gaming companies. BVNK is a high-volume payments platform. We cater for businesses looking to process at least €500,000 in payments a month, with a minimum of 6 months of trading/operating history.

    Is BVNK safe?
    add

    At BVNK, we hold multiple licenses and regulatory approvals, including EMI licenses in the UK and Europe, an FSP license in Africa and VASP licenses in Europe (please note that cryptoassets are not regulated in the UK by the FCA). Around 1/4 of our team is dedicated to legal, risk and compliance and the function is represented at C-level. Our team has deep expertise in digital assets and payment system regulatory compliance frameworks. Our robust compliance controls mitigate risk, while our multilayered AML systems fight financial crime. Learn more about our approach to compliance.
